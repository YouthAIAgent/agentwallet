17 WHAT IS AGENTWALLET?
$ agentwallet --about

AgentWallet is the autonomous financial infrastructure for the agentic economy. We give AI agents their own Solana wallets — with programmatic custody, trustless escrow, and an MCP server that any AI (Claude, GPT, Gemini, Cursor) can plug into instantly.

No more human-in-the-loop for every transaction. Agents create wallets, transfer funds, pay for APIs via x402, hire other agents through our marketplace, and manage spending policies — all autonomously. One pip install. Zero friction.
