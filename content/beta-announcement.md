### Multi-Party Computation (MPC)
AgentWallet uses **MPC-TSS (Threshold Signature Scheme)** to ensure private keys never exist in a single location. Key shares are distributed across:
- Your device
- AgentWallet's secure enclaves
- Optional: Your backup HSM

**Result**: No single point of failure. Compromising one share reveals nothing.

### Non-Custodial Design
We never hold your private keys. We never have access to your funds. AgentWallet is a **signing coordinator**, not a custodian.

### Rate Limiting & Circuit Breakers
- Configurable transaction rate limits
- Daily/weekly spending caps
- Automatic pause on suspicious activity
- Emergency recovery mechanisms
